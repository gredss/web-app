About Christian Wijaya

Christian Wijaya is a contemporary luxury fashion house based in Jakarta, rooted in refined craftsmanship, conscious design, and timeless style. Founded with a vision to redefine modern elegance, the brand blends minimalist aesthetics with sustainable innovationâ€”creating collections that are as intentional as they are sophisticated.

From our design atelier in the heart of the city to our carefully curated pieces, every element reflects our commitment to quality, ethical production, and understated luxury. We believe that true elegance transcends trends, focusing instead on creating pieces that enhance the wearer's natural confidence and style.

Our journey began with a simple philosophy: fashion should be both beautiful and responsible. This ethos drives every decision we make, from sourcing the finest materials to partnering with artisans who share our commitment to excellence and sustainability.

Elegance, defined in every detail.

Our Heritage

Established in Jakarta, Christian Wijaya represents the perfect fusion of Indonesian craftsmanship and international design sensibilities. Our brand celebrates the rich textile traditions of Indonesia while embracing contemporary fashion innovation.

Our Mission

To create timeless fashion pieces that empower individuals to express their unique style with confidence, while maintaining the highest standards of quality, sustainability, and ethical production practices.
