Sahara Taupe Velvet Loafers

IDR 10,200,000

Understated yet exquisitely refined, the Sahara Taupe Velvet Loafers are handcrafted in Portugal from upcycled deadstock velvet. These loafers feature a streamlined almond toe, memory foam insole, and leather sole crafted from vegetable-tanned remnants. Designed for the sustainable sophisticate, each pair prevents over 1 kilogram of textile waste from reaching landfills. The neutral tone ensures versatility across tailored and relaxed ensembles. Finished with a hand-stitched tonal strap, these loafers exemplify the art of ethical shoemaking.
